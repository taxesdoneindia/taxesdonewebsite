About Us

At Taxes Done, we believe that expert consulting should be accessible, reliable, and affordable for all. Based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, and serving clients across India, we specialize in tax, corporate, and assurance services that help individuals and enterprises thrive.

Our strength lies in a network of experienced and qualified professionals — including Chartered Accountants, Cost Accountants, Company Secretaries, Advocates, and industry experts — who collaborate as consultants to deliver insightful, ethical, and practical solutions tailored to each client’s needs.

Every engagement is approached with a strong commitment to understanding our clients’ businesses, ensuring trusted advice and measurable value at every step. Our services are delivered across Corporate & Regulatory Advisory, Taxation Services, and Assurance Services, encompassing entity formation and regulatory compliance, Income Tax and GST advisory and compliance, and value-added internal audits and process advisory covering operations, controls, and risk management.

Services

Corporate & Regulatory Advisory

We provide corporate and regulatory advisory services to assist businesses in establishing robust governance frameworks, ensuring regulatory compliance, and implementing effective operational processes.

Entity Formation and Corporate Advisory

  • Advisory on the selection and establishment of appropriate legal and organisational structures, including companies, LLPs, partnerships, and proprietorships
  • Incorporation and registration of entities with relevant statutory and regulatory authorities
  • Drafting, review, and standardisation of constitutional documents, partnership deeds, and internal governance documentation
  • Advisory support to start-ups and growing enterprises in establishing baseline corporate governance and compliance frameworks

Corporate and Regulatory Compliance with Process Enablement

  • Integrated corporate and regulatory compliance advisory and execution under the Companies Act, LLP Act, tax laws, labour laws, and other applicable commercial regulations
  • Ongoing compliance management, including statutory filings, disclosures, registrations, amendments, renewals, and periodic regulatory reporting
  • Advisory on regulatory interpretation, applicability, and implementation across business operations
  • Assistance in responding to statutory notices, inspections, and regulatory communications
  • Design and implementation of structured compliance processes and internal control mechanisms, including development of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), workflow documentation, and compliance monitoring frameworks

Taxation Services

We provide comprehensive direct and indirect tax advisory services, supporting clients across the tax lifecycle through structured advisory, compliance management, and dispute resolution, with a focus on risk management and regulatory certainty.

Direct Tax (Income Tax) Advisory

  • Strategic tax advisory including transaction structuring, tax planning, and optimisation for domestic and cross-border transactions
  • End-to-end income tax compliance management, including return filings, advance tax, withholding tax, and regulatory reporting
  • Tax controversy and litigation support, covering assessments, audits, appeals, rectifications, and representation before tax authorities and appellate forums
  • International taxation and NRI advisory, including residential status determination, cross-border income, transfer pricing interface, tax treaty interpretation, and FEMA-aligned compliance

Indirect Tax (Goods and Services Tax – GST) Advisory

  • Comprehensive GST advisory and compliance management, covering registrations, periodic filings, refunds, reconciliations, and statutory disclosures
  • Transaction advisory on classification, valuation, place of supply, and input tax credit optimisation
  • GST dispute resolution and litigation support, including departmental audits, investigations, assessments, and appellate proceedings
  • Ongoing GST risk assessment, health checks, and advisory on regulatory developments and impact analysis

Assurance Services

We provide independent assurance and advisory services designed to enhance governance, strengthen internal controls, and improve the effectiveness and efficiency of business operations.

Internal Audit and Risk Advisory

  • Risk-based internal audit services covering operational, financial, and compliance areas
  • Evaluation of internal control frameworks, identification of control gaps, and recommendations for remediation
  • Risk identification, assessment, and mitigation advisory across key business processes
  • Thematic and special audits, including compliance reviews, process audits, and focused operational assessments

Process Implementation and Business Advisory

  • Design and implementation of process frameworks and internal control structures aligned with business and regulatory requirements
  • Development and documentation of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for finance, tax, and operational functions
  • Review and optimisation of business processes to improve efficiency, consistency, and compliance
  • Advisory support on strengthening management reporting, monitoring mechanisms, and process governance
