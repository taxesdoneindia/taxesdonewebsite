How We Work

Compliance is not just about filing returns — it requires understanding your requirements, managing risks, and maintaining consistency. Our expert team ensures everything is handled correctly, without last-minute stress or uncertainty.

01

Understanding Your Requirements

Our expert team first understands your situation, transactions, and business activities — and clearly explains the legal and compliance requirements applicable to you before taking any action.

02

Making Compliance Clear

We identify all your compliance requirements and guide you on what needs to be done and what to avoid — so nothing is missed or misunderstood.

03

Getting the Tax Right

We guide you on structuring and tax planning — so you pay the right amount of tax, without overpaying or underpaying.

04

Handling Everything Consistently

Our team manages your filings, deadlines, and ongoing requirements — ensuring everything stays on track without last-minute stress.

05

Keeping You Informed and in Control

We keep you informed about risks, changes, and responsibilities — so you always know where you stand and stay compliant with confidence.

What We Offer

We support you across tax and compliance — from planning and structuring to filings, notices, and litigation — ensuring everything is handled correctly with complete clarity.

Income Tax & International Taxation

From planning to filing, including complex tax matters
  • Income tax return filings for individuals and businesses
  • Tax planning and advisory, including high-value and complex transactions
  • Non-resident (NRI) and international taxation support
  • Transaction structuring and tax impact analysis
  • Support in handling notices, assessments, and representations
  • Assistance across litigation and higher-level proceedings

Goods & Services Tax (GST)

Complete support across GST compliance, advisory, and issue handling
  • GST registration and setup
  • GST return filings and ongoing compliance management
  • Advisory on classification, valuation, and applicability
  • Input tax credit (ITC) eligibility and reconciliation
  • GST refund processing, including exports, inverted duty structure, and excess balances
  • Transaction structuring and GST impact analysis
  • Support in handling notices, departmental queries, and assessments

Company Law Compliance

Structured support for corporate requirements
  • Company incorporation and structuring support
  • ROC filings and ongoing compliance
  • Advisory on company law requirements and obligations
  • Assistance in handling compliance-related issues
  • Ongoing support as your business grows

Other Regulatory Compliances

Handling other applicable compliance requirements
  • Advisory on applicable legal and regulatory requirements, including labour law, FEMA, and other relevant compliances
  • Helping you understand what needs to be done and what to avoid
  • Ongoing support in managing compliances — including filings, registrations, and periodic requirements
  • Ensuring your requirements are handled on time, without confusion or missed obligations
